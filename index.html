Introduction

This project is aimed at showing public opinion in different platform. By collecting data and analysing content,  this project illustrate the difference in sentiment and public opinion between social media and news client. A quantitive content analysis of 1500 Weibo Hot Search, as well as of 1500 TopBuzz Hot Topic.

News Consumption

New Media

New media has become the main channel for the Chinese public to obtain news information. Social media represented by WeChat and TikTok are the most important new media types for news information. 

Traditional Media

Few people only get news and information from traditional media. 

Information Resources


News client and social apps occupy the first position as the most important portal for users to access information on mobile devices. Social networks were second, with their market penetration growing by 31% compared to the previous year. 

Users Age

The age group of social media and news app users

20

Younger users under the age of 20 and older users over the age of 40 show clear differences in the use of news client and social media to access information, with a clear symmetry across age groups. 

40

The lower the age group, the more users rely on social apps for information. With age, users gradually use information apps more than social.
