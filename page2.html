TopBuzz Information Matching Algorithm

How TopBuzz recommends news to you？

User

In order to provide users with content they like, or to understand their needs, there are many ways a platform can portray a user profile, such as age, gender, history of articles viewed, environmental characteristics, etc. 

Interest

Interest matching: The user's reading label and the article label overlap the most, and the article is determined to be most likely to be of interest by the system.

Batch recommendation: First, it will be recommended to a group of users who are most likely to be interested in it. The reading data generated by this group of users will play a decisive role in the next recommendation of the article. 

Content

The machine extracts keywords from the article, or uses AI technology to identify specific audio and video content to quickly classify the content.
