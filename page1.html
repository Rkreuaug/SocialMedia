Introduction

What is Weibo Hot Search?

Weibo Hot Search combined the search volume, topic, and interaction factors to calculate the real-time popularity of the Weibo and rank the top 50 topics. Weibo hot search are divided into three sections: the hot search list, the topic list, and friend search list, with the hot search list being the first to receive a lot of attention.

How it works?

The specific ranking formula of weibo hot search list is (search heat + spread heat)× topic factor × interaction factor. Search heat is based on search volume, spread heat is based on reading volume of hot blog posts, topic factor is based on discussion volume, and interaction factor is based on the interactive rate of page comments and likes.

Weibo Hot Search List Category

The top three categories in Weibo hot search are entertainment (36%), society(32%), and fashion (9%). These four hot searches account for as much as 70%. It can be seen that, compared with the news client, the content presented by Weibo hot search is more inclined to leisure and entertainment.
